About Us

Youth for Environment And Social Transformation (YEAST) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization that was established on 17th April 2022. Founded by a group of dynamic students from various institutions specializing in applied sciences, along with the guidance of senior educators as consultants, YEAST is committed to enhancing the development of character and leadership abilities in youth. The organization achieves this by organizing a variety of training programs, discussions, debate sessions, courses, and meetings, which also benefit local farmers by spreading knowledge and skills. YEAST's primary goal is to bridge the gap between rural, environmental, and social education, enabling individuals to understand issues more practically and effectively at the grassroots level. Since its inception, YEAST has been actively involved in multiple sectors, including environmental conservation, social issues, education, veterinary, and agriculture. Through its work, YEAST strives to empower youth and local communities to become change-makers who lead sustainable and socially responsible lives.
