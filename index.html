About Us

Youth for Environment And Social Transformation (YEAST) is a non-profit organization established on April 17, 2022, by dynamic students from various applied sciences institutions, with guidance from senior educators. YEAST is committed to enhancing character and leadership development in youth through training programs, discussions, debates, courses, and meetings, benefiting local farmers by disseminating knowledge and skills. The organization's primary goal is to bridge the gap between rural, environmental, and social education, enabling individuals to understand issues more practically and effectively at the grassroots level. Since its inception, YEAST has been actively involved in multiple sectors, including environmental conservation, social issues, education, veterinary, and agriculture. Through its work, YEAST strives to empower youth and local communities to become change-makers who lead sustainable and socially responsible lives.
