About Us

Youth for Environment And Social Transformation (YEAST) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization founded on April 17, 2022, by a group of dynamic students from various institutions, in collaboration with senior educators. Our mission is to empower youth, enhance their leadership skills, and promote social and environmental sustainability in Nepal. We believe that youth play a crucial role in shaping the future of our society, and through YEAST, we aim to foster their growth as change-makers and leaders. Our organization focuses on bridging the gap between environmental and social education, grassroots-level awareness, and the pressing challenges faced by local communities. YEAST operates in several key areas: -Environmental Conservation: We engage in environmental awareness programs, advocacy campaigns, and sustainability initiatives to protect natural resources. - Social Development: Our initiatives address social justice, youth empowerment, and community development through training, workshops, and outreach activities. - Agricultural Education: We aim to introduce sustainable farming practices to rural farmers, helping them improve productivity and livelihoods in an eco-friendly manner. - Veterinary and Health: YEAST works on promoting animal welfare and healthcare in rural areas, ensuring the health and well-being of animals and communities alike. - We conduct various programs, including discussions, debate sessions, workshops, and leadership training, to equip youth with the necessary skills and knowledge to make a positive impact. Our members and volunteers come from diverse backgrounds, but they all share a common commitment to creating a sustainable and equitable society. Through our work, we aspire to create a world where young people are empowered to lead efforts in environmental conservation, social transformation, and community development. We welcome the active participation of youth, educators, and community members in shaping this vision.
