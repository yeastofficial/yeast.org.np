About Us

Youth for Environment And Social Transformation (YEAST) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization established in 2022 with the aim to empower the youth to actively participate in environmental conservation and social development. We engage in various sectors including agriculture, education, and social justice to promote sustainable practices and awareness.
