About Us

YEAST is a platform for youth to become change-makers in environmental conservation and social development. Our mission is to bridge gaps in education, environmental awareness, and sustainable practices in Nepal.

Our Projects

Cleanliness Campaign

Organizing community-driven cleanup drives to promote hygiene and environmental conservation.

Sustainable Agriculture

Implementing sustainable farming practices to support local farmers and protect the environment.

Youth Leadership Training

Empowering young leaders with the skills to create meaningful change in their communities.
